Welcome

Welcome and thank you for visiting our new website. Please feel free to contact us for any additional information. Forestville United Methodist Church is open to all and actively responds to the needs of people in the west Sonoma County. We invite you to explore making Forestville your spiritual home.

Services are held in the main Church: Sunday Morning services are at 8:30 AM and 10:30 AM.Any additional services will be announced on our notice board and on our website.
